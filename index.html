Essential Care Tips

Daily Exercise

Regular physical activity is crucial for your dog's physical and mental health. Most dogs need 30-60 minutes of exercise daily.

Regular Grooming

Brush your dog regularly, trim nails, clean ears, and maintain dental hygiene to keep them healthy and comfortable.

Proper Nutrition

Feed high-quality dog food appropriate for your dog's age, size, and activity level. Always provide fresh water.

Veterinary Care

Schedule regular check-ups, keep vaccinations current, and address health concerns promptly with your vet.
